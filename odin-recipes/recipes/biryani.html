Chicken Biryani

Description

Marinate the Chicken: In a mixing bowl, combine the chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, and salt. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

Prepare the Rice: Wash the basmati rice under running water until the water runs clear. In a large pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add the washed rice, green cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon stick, and salt. Cook the rice until it's 70-80% cooked (it should still have a slight bite to it). Drain the water and set the rice aside.

Cook the Chicken: In a large, heavy-bottomed pan, heat the oil or ghee over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove some for garnish if desired. Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook until it's no longer pink and starts to brown. Add the chopped tomatoes, green chilies, and half of the chopped coriander and mint leaves. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the chicken is cooked through. If you like, you can sprinkle some biryani masala at this stage for extra flavor.

Layering the Biryani: In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, layer half of the partially cooked rice at the bottom. Add the cooked chicken mixture on top of the rice. Sprinkle the remaining coriander and mint leaves over the chicken. Layer the remaining rice on top of the chicken.

Dum Cooking (Slow Cooking): Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid or seal it with aluminum foil. Cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes. You can also place a heavy pan or tava (griddle) under the biryani pot to distribute heat evenly. Check for doneness by inserting a fork into the biryani. If it comes out clean, it's ready.

Garnish and Serve: Garnish the chicken biryani with fried onions and saffron milk (if using). Serve hot with raita (yogurt dip) or a side salad.

Ingredients for Chicken Marination
  • 500g bonless chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Salt to taste
Ingredients for Rice
  • 1 Cup Basmati Rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste
Ingredients for Biryani
  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 2-3 green hilies
  • 1/2 teaspoon biryani masala
  • Saffrom strands
