Chicken Karahi

Description

Instructions

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a karahi or a deep, heavy-bottomed pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent and slightly browned.
  3. Add the ginger and garlic paste, and sauté for another minute until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and fry them until they turn white on all sides.
  5. Stir in the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  6. Add the green chilies, red chili powder, turmeric powder, ground cumin, and ground coriander. Mix well.
  7. Cover the pan and let the chicken cook on low to medium heat for about 15-20 minutes, or until it's cooked through and tender. Be sure to stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  8. Check the seasoning and add salt to taste. Adjust the spice level by adding more red chili powder if desired.
  9. Garnish the chicken karahi with fresh coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot with naan or roti.

Ingredients
  • 500g of Chicken
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 2-3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili poweder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
